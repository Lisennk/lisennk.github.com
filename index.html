Hi, my name is Taras Pohrebniak.

My passion is in AI and digital mental health.

My "Why"

In 2017, I went through a severe depression for the first time. I discovered that it's not one of those challenges that "make us stronger". It makes you the weakest, without providing anything in return.

Overall, 67.5% of people who suffer from mental health issues do not get help. I want to solve this problem and help everyone who fights the darkness.

Currently I am CEO at Elomia — our long-term goal is to create an AI-powered companion with ability to provide therapy with a human-level effectiveness.

It's my second startup on the mental health market. Right now we almost finished our tech and trying to get Product-Market-Fit.

DreamSpace ↗

My Other Products

Poetry AI ↗

Secret Chat ↗
